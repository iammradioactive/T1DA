Think Before You Click

Warning: You have clicked on a simulated phishing link

This is part of a training exercise to create awareness about phishing threats. Always remember to be vigilant and cautious with links and emails from known or unknown sources.

Here are the some tips to help you identify phishing attempts

  • Verify the source: Always check the sender's details and look for suspicious email addresses or misspelled domains.
  • Don't share personal information: Never provide sensitive personal or financial information in response to an email request.
  • Use security software: Keep your devices protected with updated antivirus and anti-phishing software.
